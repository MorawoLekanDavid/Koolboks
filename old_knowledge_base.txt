## HOW SOLAR WORKS
Sun -> Panels -> Pedestal Battery -> Freezer.
Battery lasts 8-12hrs without sun depending on battery size and usage.
Charge time: 6-8 hours under good sunlight.
Power unit 24-48hrs first to activate Ice Battery.
Ice Battery keeps cold 3-4 DAYS without any power. Set thermostat max to 3.
System can also be connected to NEPA or a generator as an additional charging source.
